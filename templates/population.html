HIV+ population refers to people living with HIV and/or AIDS. New HIV infections refers to all new cases of HIV diagnosed in the city of San Francisco.

The HIV+ population data illustrated on this page was collected by the San Francisco Department of Public Health HIV Prevention Section. People living with HIV/AIDS statistics can be found on page 15 in Table 3.1 of the 2010 HIV Prevention Section Annual Report. Metics of new HIV infections can be found on Page 1, Table 1.1 of the HIV Prevention Section Annual Report.

San Francisco population statistics are from the 2010 US Census.
